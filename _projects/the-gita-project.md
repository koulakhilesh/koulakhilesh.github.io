The Gita Project is a verse-by-verse digital study of the Bhagavad Gita. All 18 chapters and 700 shlokas (verses) are in place, each with the Sanskrit, a transliteration, a word-by-word gloss, and a translation.

I'm still working on it. What's coming: visualisations of the text (who speaks, how the dialogue shifts, which words recur), notes on the themes, and a handful of posts that dig into what 700 verses look like when you map them out.

More to follow. Check back later.
